This is a guide for "Generators". "Generators" is a technical mod, aiming to simplify, alleviate and speed up various activities in the game. Most of the blocks and items it adds require energy to operate, but there are exceptions. Currently Generators use energy API "Energies", which is open-source and ready for use by other mods.

Most of the objects require Energetic Ingot in crafting; its recipe is -

  • Energy generators
  • Blocks that use energy
  • Items that use energy
  • Blocks that don't use energy
  • Companion Spheres
Generators adds a tool enchantment "Multi-break", which works like vein-mining.
