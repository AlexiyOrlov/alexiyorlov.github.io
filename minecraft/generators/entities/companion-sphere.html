Companion Spheres are entities that can be created by player and be given different tasks. Currently there are 4 tasks implemented:
  1. Follow - simply follow the owner
  2. Protect owner - attack mobs that attack the owner or are attacked by owner
  3. Guard area - guards an area of a specified range from hostile mobs
  4. Harvest assist - assists the player in block harvesting
A sphere will protect itself from aggressors automatically.

Now, if you are interested in them, I will show the process of creating a sphere.

First, you will need four Focus Blocks:

Then, you have to place them at 11 blocks apart from each other, so there is an imaginary cross inside (lapis and red wool placed for reference):

If you hear a beacon activation sound when you place the last Focus block, then you placed them all correctly.

Next, you have to choose how big sphere you want. A sphere size can be from 1 to 5, the bigger the size, the higher is its health and damage. You determine that by placing one of the following blocks in the center (from less valuable to more valuable): Iron block, Steel block, Gold block, Diamond block or Emerald block.

Then the Focus blocks are ready to accept energy - supply it for all of them; once the energy is being supplied, the particles show that sphere creation is in progress (here I used Lunar panels):

Once all Focus blocks are full of energy, the offered block will be destroyed and a sphere with a random color will be spawned instead. Right click the sphere's center to claim it.

After this you can open Sphere's GUI by right-clicking its center again to see some info and/or set a task:

Lastly, know that spheres has no clip and are not affected by gravity - they can go anywhere through any blocks; they attack mobs with a short beam.
