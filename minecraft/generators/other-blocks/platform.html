Platform is a block which is used by a Pusher to move adjacent blocks. You can configure which sides will grab the adjacent blocks by a wrench or empty hand:

Yellow color means the side is open and will grab a block, pale color means the side is closed and won't grab a block. These colors can be changed in the settings.
