Functions of this wrench:
  1. info about energy handlers - right-click on an energy block and it will tell you the block's energy type, capacity, and input/output rates.
  2. info about energy requests in a cable network: right-click a cable, and it will tell which energy consumer requires most energy; if sneaking, will list all consumers that require energy.
  3. rotating blocks.
