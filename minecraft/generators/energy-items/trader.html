Trader is an item for convenient and quick trading with villagers.

Right-clicking the trader will trigger a scan of 130x130 block area around the player for villagers that have trades. If it finds villager(s) with trades, a screen will open which will list them, their positions, professions, ids and levels:

The list is scrollable by mouse wheel; press appropriate trade button to start trading with corresponding villager.
