Translocator is an item which can transport a block or an entity to anywhere. It works by right-clicking on a block you want to move - if the translocator has enough energy, it will store the block (this operation costs 1000 EU + 1000 more if the block has a block entity); you can then put it anywhere you want.

Moving entities is different - click on an entity with Translocator, and it will be placed on your head (costs 500 EU). When you arrive at desired location, click it again, and the entity will be released there.
