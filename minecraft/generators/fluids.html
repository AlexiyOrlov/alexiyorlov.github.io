There are three fluids in the mod: Petroleum, Kerosene and Jet Fuel. Petroleum lakes can be found in deserts, badlands and nether. Currently its purpose is to be converted to Kerosene. Kerosene is produced in Petroleum Refinery; it can be processed further in Kerosene Refinery to produce Jet Fuel. Jet Fuel is being used by the Jetpack.

Only way to transport these fluids are to use Cans and Canisters. Can is an item which can hold 1 block of fluid (1000 units); Canister is a block that can hold 10000 units of fluid. To transport petroleum from its lake to your location, place a canister nearby and fill it using the can on the petroleum blocks. When finished, the canister can be safely broken, because it will save its fluid.

I might add more uses for these fluids in future - stay tuned.
