Crop Handler automatically harvests and replants crops in a standard 9x9 area of farmland.

It must be placed at the side of the farmland area, facing the row with the water source. It will harvest all regular crops (wheat, potatoes, beetroot, carrots, pumpkins, melons and their derivatives) and the nether wart.
